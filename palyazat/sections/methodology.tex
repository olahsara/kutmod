\noindent
The project follows a multi-phase methodology:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Data Collection:} 3D scanning and photogrammetry of selected sites
    \item \textbf{Data Processing:} Creation of high-resolution digital models
    \item \textbf{XR Integration:} Development of VR/AR environments
    \item \textbf{Validation:} Testing in real-world and user scenarios
\end{enumerate}

Special attention is given to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Outdoor scanning challenges (lighting, terrain, weather)
    \item Standardization of data collection protocols
    \item Interoperability of datasets
\end{itemize}
