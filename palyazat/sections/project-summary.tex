// Sári innen
\noindent
The project focuses on the digital preservation and accessibility of cultural heritage sites using XR technologies. By integrating 3D scanning, photogrammetry, and immersive VR/AR environments, it enables the creation of digital twins of endangered monuments.

Two major challenges are addressed:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Physical degradation caused by mass tourism and environmental factors
    \item Limited accessibility to remote or fragile heritage sites
\end{itemize}

The project operates across Mediterranean, Central-Eastern European, and Atlantic regions, combining technological innovation with cultural heritage expertise.
