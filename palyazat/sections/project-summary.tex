\noindent
The project focuses on the digital preservation and accessibility of cultural heritage sites using XR technologies. By integrating 3D scanning, photogrammetry, and immersive VR/AR environments, we aim to create digital twins of endangered monuments.

The initiative addresses two key challenges:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Physical degradation due to mass tourism and environmental factors
    \item Limited accessibility to remote or fragile heritage sites
\end{itemize}

The project operates across multiple European regions (Mediterranean, Central-Eastern Europe, Atlantic), combining technological innovation with cultural heritage expertise.
