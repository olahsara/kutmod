
\noindent
The project relies on a comprehensive network of specialized infrastructures provided by the consortium:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{CAPLAB Platform (Arts et Métiers / Laval Virtual):} A European hub for XR, digital twins, and 3D technologies, providing robust infrastructure for 4D reality capture and technology transfer.
    \item \textbf{Digital Open Science Ecosystem (Springer Nature):} The platform infrastructure for hosting and distributing open access articles, datasets, 3D models, and VR assets.
    \item \textbf{Mediterranean Field Sites (Sapienza \& NKUA):} Access to highly vulnerable urban UNESCO sites in Rome and weather-exposed open-air ruins in Greece, serving as the physical infrastructure for technological testing and validation.
    \item \textbf{Digital Humanities Network (ELTE BTK):} The National Laboratory for Digital Heritage and the Danube-AI initiative in Hungary, supplying the computational and methodological infrastructure for AI-driven heritage processing.
    \item \textbf{Data Collection Network (Sorbonne Université):} Established infrastructure for coordinating architectural and historical documentation, particularly concerning fortifications and coastal heritage.
\end{itemize}
