\noindent
The consortium consists of interdisciplinary partners:

\textbf{Technology Partner:}
Arts et Métiers (CAPLAB / Laval Virtual) – XR technologies and 4D data capture

\textbf{Publishing Partner:}
Springer Nature – Open access dissemination and scientific publishing

\textbf{Mediterranean Region:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Sapienza Università di Roma – archaeology and heritage theory
    \item National and Kapodistrian University of Athens – outdoor heritage and risk analysis
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Central-Eastern Europe:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE) – digital humanities and AI-based heritage processing
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Atlantic Region:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Sorbonne Université – data collection coordination and historical architecture
\end{itemize}
