\noindent
The market potential and exploitation strategies are deeply intertwined with the open science model and XR sector expansion:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Open Access Revenue Model:} The partnership with Springer Nature leverages Article Processing Charges (APCs) to ensure free access to publications, 3D models, and VR assets. This attracts not only academics but also software developers and XR startups, leading to higher download rates and increased brand reputation.
    \item \textbf{XR Technology Expansion:} Validating 4D capture in complex cultural heritage scenarios opens a completely new market segment for XR technologies, increasing their societal relevance and commercial viability beyond traditional industrial/health training.
    \item \textbf{Sustainable Tourism Economy:} By addressing the physical destruction caused by overtourism, the project provides a scalable digital alternative. "Experience-replicas" and remote access tools offer tourism boards new digital products that generate engagement while unburdening fragile UNESCO sites.
\end{itemize}
